WHAT IS AOS?
------------
AOS (Abhigyan Operating System) is a kernel developed from scratch by Oppeko.
It is designed for the x86_64 architecture and follows a modular, 
efficient design pattern.

THE PHILOSOPHY
--------------
The motive behind AOS is simple yet powerful:
"If you bought your hardware, why pay to use it?"

AOS aims to eliminate the "software tax" and "bloat" often found in modern
operating systems. It is open-sourced under the GPL 3.0 license, ensuring
that the community always has access to the code.

MEET THE DEVELOPER
------------------
AOS is the brainchild of Abhigyan Narayan (Oppeko). 
Passionate about low-level systems and hardware freedom, Abhigyan has
crafted this kernel to prove that a modern, functional OS can be 
built without the unnecessary complexity of mainstream alternatives.

THE VISION: MUI (Mastered User Interface)
-----------------------------------------
Inspired by Dragon Ball's "Ultra Instinct" (Mastered Self-Movement), 
MUI is the AOS vision for a user interface that is fluid, powerful, 
and "just works." It's more than just a GUI; it's a philosophy of 
interaction.
