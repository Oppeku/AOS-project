What is AOS?

AOS (Abhigyan Operating System) is a kernel developed from scratch by Oppeko.

The Philosophy

"If you bought your hardware, why pay to use it?"

AOS aims to eliminate the "software tax" and "bloat" often found in modern operating systems. It is open-sourced under the GPL 3.0 license.

Meet the Developer

AOS is the brainchild of Abhigyan Narayan (Oppeko). Passionate about low-level systems, Abhigyan has crafted this kernel to prove that a modern, functional OS can be built without unnecessary complexity.

The Vision: MUI

MUI stands for Mastered User Interface. Inspired by Dragon Ball's "Ultra Instinct," MUI is our vision for a user interface that is fluid, powerful, and efficient.
